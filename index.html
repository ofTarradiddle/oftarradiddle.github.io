Accelerating health discovery through curated research via "social trials" i.e. Clinical Trials through a social medium.

We are building out a decentrailzied clinical trial platform that allows you to study the impact of your actions on specific outcomes of interest through tracking structured inputs and outputs across a large number of participants. By opening up clinical trials to the masses we reboot the traditional system of clinical trials that is costly and leaves decision making out of the individuals hands. Join or Create your own study, and help contribute to open research on self-administered clinical trials.
